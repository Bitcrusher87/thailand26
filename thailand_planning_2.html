Must order — Northern Thai classics:

  • Northern appetizer platter — best way to try everything at once
  • Sai ua — herbed pork sausage fragrant with lemongrass and galangal

Thu Jun 11 · Day 20
Bangkok — Last Full Day

One last day in Bangkok. Fill it however feels right after 18 days of temples, mountains and islands.

A few ideas depending on energy:

  • One more cultural hit: Wat Arun (Temple of Dawn) across the river — stunning tiled spires, less crowded than the Grand Palace, best in morning light. Take the short ferry from Tha Tien pier near Wat Pho.
  • Last shop: Or Tor Kor Market near Chatuchak — the best fresh produce and food market in Bangkok. Or ICONSIAM on the river for a final mall experience with incredible food hall.
  • Pure relaxation: Thai massage (anywhere in Sukhumvit, ±300–500 THB/hr), pool, cold drinks. You've earned it.
  • Best street food finale: Jay Fai — Bangkok's most famous cook, Michelin-starred crab omelette on Mahachai Road. Queue early or book weeks ahead online.
💡 Pack your checked bags tonight — tomorrow morning must be stress-free. Flight is at 12:05 so leave the hotel by 09:00. Keep only overnight essentials in your backpack.
